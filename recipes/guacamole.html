Guacamole

Description

A creamy, zingy, low calorie side dish perfect for taco night!

Ingredients

For 4 servings:

  • 3 avocados - peeled, pitted and mashed
  • 1 lime - juiced
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup diced onion
  • 3 tbsp freshly chopped cilantro
  • 2 roma/plum tomatoes - diced
  • 1 tsp minced garlic
  • A pinch of ground cayenne pepper

Steps

  1. In a medium bowl, mash together the avocados, lime juice, and salt.

  2. Mix in onion, cilantro, tomatoes, and garlic.

  3. Stir in cayenne pepper. Refrigerate 1 hour for best flavor, or serve immediately.
