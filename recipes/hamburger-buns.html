Bert's Hamburger Buns

Description

"My mum used to make these buns all the time at barbecues and parties - safe to say everyone enjoyed them! So much so that they refused to use the store-bought ones and called my poor mother into the kitchen to make them!" - Bert Raye.

Ever had a burger and realised halfway through that it'd be so much more satisfying to eat without the soggy bottom bun that crumbles apart? Well look no further (or actually, do look further down the page!) as this recipe provides all you need to know on how to create the perfect, structurally sound burger buns!

Ingredients

For 12 servings:

  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 cup water
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp white sugar
  • 1 1/2 tsp salt
  • 5 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 (.25) oz envelope active dry yeast
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 1 tbsp water
